SPORTS

BASKETBALL

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete with the primary objective of shooting a basketball through the defender's hoop while preventing the opposing team from shooting through their own hoop. TENNIS

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent or between two teams of two players each.
